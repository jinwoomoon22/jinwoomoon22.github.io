Senior Summer in KL

June 6 ~ July 15

Took a grab back to Mont' Kiara where Aoi bought brunch at this fancy cafe. Spent around 3 hours discussing about Mei.

June 21: Finally meeting as a whole group with the gang while eating Hotpot at Solaris. We didn't do much except go Karaoke and go to a new bar afterwards. Me and KC refused to drink because it was way too expensive.

Senior Summer in Seoul

July 16 ~ August 12

He had slimmed down a considerable amount, first unable to recognize him. We ate some galbi together, and then we went to his house played some video games, ate ice cream, and did some karaoke.
